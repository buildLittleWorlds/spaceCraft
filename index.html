Why This Exists

Hugging Face Spaces hosts thousands of machine learning demos, but the conversation around them is almost entirely about model performance — benchmarks, parameter counts, eval scores. That matters, but it's not the whole story.

Some Spaces are genuinely fun to use. They have thoughtful interfaces, inventive features, and the kind of visual polish that makes you want to share them with someone. That craft deserves recognition.

SpaceCraft rates Spaces on three axes: Visual Craft (does it look good?), Feature Innovation (does it do something you haven't seen?), and Delight Factor (do you want to keep playing with it?). Each is scored 1–10, and the total is out of 30.

The goal is simple: encourage the HF community to treat Spaces as a real platform for creative, polished, delightful applications — not just model wrappers. Free hosting should not mean boring interfaces.

Built as a teaching resource for university students learning to build on Hugging Face. Contributions and nominations welcome.
