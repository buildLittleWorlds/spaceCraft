Research Methods Gallery

Eight ways to investigate AI systems. Each method is a tool you can use on any model or Space — not just the ones in this course.

These methods come from real research practice. Scientists, engineers, and product designers use versions of these every day. The difference is that you're applying them to Hugging Face Spaces you can test right now, for free.

Each method page shows you the steps, maps them to Spaces on the leaderboard, and gives you an exercise to try. Read them in order or jump to whichever one your current project needs.
